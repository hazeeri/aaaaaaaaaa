Untitled.


Untitled— is a workshop where we encourage teens and netizens to spread positivity throughout the internet.

Our goal— is to spread awareness regarding this type of bullying going on in the internet, most people call this as Cyberbullying.

Our team— offers great hospitality, positive and open-minded environment for our participants to feel free expressing themselves with each one of us.
